SELECT B.MEMBER_NAME, A.REVIEW_TEXT, DATE_FORMAT(A.REVIEW_DATE, '%Y-%m-%d') AS REVIEW_DATE
FROM REST_REVIEW A
JOIN MEMBER_PROFILE B ON B.MEMBER_ID = A.MEMBER_ID
WHERE A.MEMBER_ID = (
                    SELECT MEMBER_ID
                    FROM REST_REVIEW
                    GROUP BY MEMBER_ID
                    ORDER BY COUNT(MEMBER_ID) DESC LIMIT 1 )
ORDER BY REVIEW_DATE, REVIEW_TEXT   


- A가 가장많은 B의 C,D,F를 가져오기
* 서브쿼리로 가장많은 카운트의 MEMBER_ID를 가져옴
